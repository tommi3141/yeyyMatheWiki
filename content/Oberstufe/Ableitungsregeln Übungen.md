> [!note]+ Aufgabe 1
> # Produktregel
> 1. $f(x) = x \cdot \sin(x)$  
> 2. $f(x) = x \cdot e^x$  
> 3. $f(x) = x^2 \cdot \ln(x)$  
> 4. $f(x) = x^3 \cdot \cos(x)$  
> 5. $f(x) = x \cdot \sqrt{x}$
> 6. $f(x) = x^3 \cdot e^{-x}$ 
> 
>> [!success]- LÃķsung anzeigen:
>> 1. $f'(x) = x \cdot \cos(x) + \sin(x)$  
>> 2. $f'(x) = x \cdot e^x + e^x$  
>> 3. $f'(x) = x + 2x \cdot \ln(x)$  
>> 4. $f'(x) = -x^3 \cdot \sin(x) + 3x^2 \cdot \cos(x)$  
>> 5. $f'(x) = \frac{x}{2\sqrt{x}} + \sqrt{x} = \frac{3}{2}\sqrt{x}$
>> 6. $f'(x) = 3x^2 \cdot e^{-x} - x^3 \cdot e^{-x} = (3x^2 - x^3) \cdot e^{-x}$


> [!note]+ Aufgabe 1

> 1. $f(x) = \sin(x^2)$  
> 2. $f(x) = \cos(x^2)$  
> 3. $f(x) = e^{x^2}$  
> 4. $f(x) = \sqrt{5x+1}$  
> 5. $f(x) = (2x+3)^4$ 
> 6. $f(x) = \ln(x^4+2)$
> 7. $f(x) = e^{-\sqrt{x}}$
> 
>> [!success]- LÃķsung anzeigen:
>> 1. $f'(x) = \cos(x^2) \cdot 2x$  
>> 2. $f'(x) = -\sin(x^2) \cdot 2x$  
>> 3. $f'(x) = e^{x^2} \cdot 2x$  
>> 4. $f'(x) = \frac{5}{2\sqrt{5x+1}}$  
>> 5. $f'(x) = 8 \cdot (2x+3)^3$ 
>> 6. $f'(x) = \frac{1}{x^4 + 2} \cdot 4x^3 = \frac{4x^3}{x^4 + 2}$ 
>> 7. $f'(x) = e^{-\sqrt{x}} \cdot \left(-\frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}\right) = -\frac{e^{-\sqrt{x}}}{2\sqrt{x}}$
